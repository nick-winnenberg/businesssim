Market Research Report

Welcome to the Food Truck Rally - we are very glad you are here! The goal of this simulation is to sell meals to hungry patrons. First - we should discuss the different demographics that you'll be selling to in this scenerio!

Demographics are made of preferences and sensitivities

Preferences

Preferences are the desired food types of each demographics. The higher the preference, the more of that food type the demographic will purchase!

Sensitivites

Sensitivites measure how responsive the demographic is to certain strategies. For example, if a demographic has a high price-sensitivey, the'll respond more positively to low-price food. Likewise, if they have a high quality sensitivity, they'll respond well to high quality meals.

Strategy

When reviewing the following demographics, start to think... Which food type do I want to sell, and what strategy can I deploy to maximize my business.

Important Note: You will select your food type in the next step. You will NOT select a demographic. You will have customers from ALL demographics buying meals from you, so it's important to think of the market as a whole, when deciding your strategy.

Example Strategy: Sell High-Quality Italian Food, while focusing on large margins, and online marketing to target two demographics that are highly quality and online marketing sensitive.

Read the demographic information below. When you are confident with your big-picture strategy, press the button below!"
